Welcome to My "Through the Lens Of Family Site Plan"



This website is a visual portfolio that showcases my passion for photography, focusing on the beauty of family connections. Each photograph tells a unique story, revealing love, bond, and individuality. I aim to highlight the beauty in diverse family dynamics, allowing viewers to experience the genuine emotions shared in these timeless moments.

Purpose and Audience

The purpose of this website is to showcase my photography portfolio, emphasizing the beauty and uniqueness of family connections. My intended audience is families who are seeking to capture their special moments through photography, as well as individuals who appreciate the art of family storytelling through visuals.

Description of Dynamic Elements

I will use JavaScript to add dynamic elements to my website, such as quote boxes that display family-related quotes dynamically.

Logo

I created a logo with a camera icon and my name on top of the lens. It's a simple, clean design that reflects my photography style.

Colors

Fonts

The fonts chosen for this website reflect a modern, and clean feel that aligns with the theme of family photography. Here are the two primary fonts used:

  • Primary Font: 'Lato' - Used for body text and normal paragraphs, providing a clean, modern, and highly readable style.
  • Secondary Font: 'Satisfy' - Used for headings (H1 and H2) to give a creative, elegant, and cursive touch to the website.
