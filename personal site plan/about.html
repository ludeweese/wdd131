About the Artist

Lucilene DeWeese has loved photography since she was a young girl. Her passion grew even stronger after two life-changing events: The sudden loss of her mom and, two years later, the arrival of her daughter.

Although her daughter will never get to meet her grandmother, Lucilene finds comfort in the photos her mom left behind. They’re more than just pictures; they’re memories filled with love and connection. This experience inspired Lucilene to dedicate herself to capturing family portraits, preserving the special bonds and stories that make each family unique.

About the Series

Through the Lens of Family is a heartfelt collection that celebrates the unique bonds and special moments that make each family story meaningful. Lucilene’s photography captures her belief that every moment, big or small, deserves to be cherished and remembered for generations.

Each image reflects her passion for showcasing the connections, growth, and love that define a family. This series is a gentle reminder to nurture those relationships and treasure the memories they create.
